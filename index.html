🎭 What is BOTC?

Good players must find and execute the Demon. Evil players protect the Demon while spreading misinformation.

🌙 Night

Everyone closes their eyes. The Storyteller wakes characters in order and resolves abilities.

☀️ Day

Discuss, bluff, share information, accuse players and hold private conversations.

🗳️ Nominations & Voting

Players nominate someone for execution. Everyone may vote. Usually only one execution happens each day.

💀 Death

  • Dead players still talk.
  • Dead players still help their team.
  • Each dead player has one Ghost Vote.

🏆 Winning

Good: Execute the Demon.

Evil: Keep the Demon alive until only two players remain.

🎒 Before You Arrive

  • Read today's script.
  • Don't worry about memorizing.
  • Ask questions.
  • Have fun!

❓ FAQ

Can I lie?

Yes. Everyone can lie.

Do I stop playing after I die?

No. You stay in the game and receive one Ghost Vote.

Do I need to know every role?

No. Read the script once and the Storyteller will explain the rest.

Can I have private conversations?

Yes, and they're a big part of the game.
