Learn the Game

A quick guide for first-timers and returning players.

Good

Find and execute the Demon before evil wins.

Evil

Protect the Demon and sow confusion until only two players remain.

🌙

Night

Everyone closes their eyes. The Storyteller wakes characters in order and resolves their abilities in secret.

☀️

Day

Open discussion. Share (or fake) information, form alliances, and hold private side conversations.

🗳️

Nominations & Voting

Any living player can nominate someone for execution. Everyone votes — usually only one execution per day.

💀

Death

  • Dead players keep talking.
  • Dead players still help their team.
  • Each dead player gets one Ghost Vote.
🏆

Winning

Good wins by executing the Demon. Evil wins if the Demon survives until only two players are left.

FAQ

Common questions from new players.

Can I lie?

Yes — everyone can lie, including about their role and the information they share. That's core to the game.

Do I stop playing after I die?

No. You stay in the game, keep talking, and get one Ghost Vote you can use on any future execution.

Do I need to know every role?

No. Skim the script once before game night. The Storyteller will explain anything unusual before play starts.

Can I have private conversations?

Absolutely — whispering and pulling people aside is a big part of the game. Just don't leave the room.

What does the Storyteller do?

The Storyteller runs the game — waking roles at night, resolving abilities, and keeping everything fair and fun.

How long does a game take?

Usually 1–2 hours depending on player count and how much everyone talks. Plan for a full evening.
